Barred Spiral Galaxy

Barred spiral galaxies share the same features and functions as regular spiral galaxies, but they also have a bar
of bright stars that lie along the center of the bulge, and extend into the disk. The bright bulge has very little
activity here and contains mostly older, red stars. The bar and arms have lots of activity.including star formation.

While the classification for barred spirals is the same as it is for regular spiral galaxies, the bar must be considered
as well. Short bars correlate to tighter galaxies and will be included in the designation SBa. SBb have longer bars and
SBc are the longest. Most astronomers now agree that the Milky Way is a barred spiral galaxy.
